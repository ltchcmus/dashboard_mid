# Prompt Log - Single Click And Header Padding

## Objective
Sua 2 van de UI/UX: header con che nhe noi dung va co che click chart can click 2 lan moi thay doi bo loc.

## Context
- Header da duoc tang do cao de chua title trong thanh do.
- Noi dung ben duoi van bi cham nhe, can them padding top.
- Cross-filter chart da co, nhung hanh vi cap nhat bo loc cho cam giac can click lan 2.

## Final Prompt
"Tang them padding top duoi header, va dieu chinh co che click chart de click 1 lan la apply bo loc ngay."

## Expected Output
- Khong con hien tuong text bi che boi header.
- Click vao diem/quoc gia/the loai/ngon ngu tren chart chi 1 lan la dashboard loc ngay.
